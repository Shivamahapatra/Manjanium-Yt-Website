# Obsidian

[[Obsidian]] is a powerful markdown-based knowledge management tool. In the context of an [[LLM_Wiki]], Obsidian acts as the IDE (Integrated Development Environment) while the LLM acts as the programmer. 

## Useful Plugins & Features
- **Graph View**: Essential for visualizing the shape of the wiki, finding hubs, and identifying orphans.
- **Web Clipper**: Useful for converting web articles into markdown sources.
- **Dataview**: Generates dynamic tables by querying YAML frontmatter added by the LLM.
- **Marp**: Allows generating slide decks directly from wiki content.
