# Manjanium On Softs

[[Manjanium_On_Softs]] is a high-performance, multi-sport telemetry and fan engagement platform. It operates as a monorepo containing multiple apps and shared packages (see [[Monorepo_Architecture]]).

## Core Features
- **[[F1_Hub]]**: Racing telemetry, live timing tower, race tracker, and pit wall simulation.
- **[[Football_Center]]**: Score center, league standings, and live match data.
- **[[Manjanium_OS_Terminal]]**: An inline CLI chat interface blended directly into the data streams.
- **[[Paddock_Simulator]]**: A 3D multiplayer racing simulator built into the platform.

## Design
The platform utilizes the [[Stitch_Design_System]] to maintain a premium "telemetry" aesthetic with glassmorphism, advanced motion layers, and dual-mode accessibility.
